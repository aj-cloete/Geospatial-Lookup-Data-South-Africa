# geonames
- feature_code
  - ADM1: Province
  - ADM2: District/Metropole
  - ADM3: City/Town
  - PPL: Populated place
- feature_class
  - A: country, state, region,...
  - H: stream, lake, ...
  - L: parks,area, ...
  - P: city, village,...
  - R: road, railroad 
  - S: spot, building, farm
  - T: mountain,hill,rock,... 
  - U: undersea
  - V: forest,heath,...
